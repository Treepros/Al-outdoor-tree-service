FAQs AND ANSWERS

What’s the benefit of tree trimming?
Tree trimming promotes healthy growth, improves the shape of your trees, and reduces the risk of falling branches. It also enhances your landscape’s aesthetic appeal.
Can tree trimming damage my tree?
When done correctly, tree trimming actually benefits your tree by removing dead or weak branches, encouraging healthy growth. Our certified arborists ensure your trees are trimmed properly.
How do I know if my tree needs trimming?
If you notice overgrown branches, dead limbs, or limbs growing too close to power lines or your home, it’s time for trimming. We offer free assessments to evaluate your tree’s needs.
